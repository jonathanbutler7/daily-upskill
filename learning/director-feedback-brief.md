# Career Conversation Notes

I am building toward deeper payments expertise and stronger technical leadership. I am also curious about management and eventually becoming a Director, but I want to understand the job before I commit to that path.

## Projects I am using to learn

- **PayerSync:** healthcare-payment pipeline for remittance and virtual-card files that arrive separately. It matches them, processes the payment, writes back results, and records each step.
- **LedgerDB:** Go/Postgres double-entry ledger. I have worked through balanced entries, insufficient funds, idempotency, external transfers, and reversals.
- **Next:** a reconciliation engine that compares the ledger with settlement reports and tracks exceptions such as missing payments, duplicates, and amount mismatches.

The common thread is learning how payment systems behave when records arrive late, get retried, disagree with each other, or need to be explained later.
